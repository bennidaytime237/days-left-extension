# Privacy Policy for Days Left in Year

**Last updated:** January 30, 2025

## Overview
Days Left in Year is a Chrome extension that displays how many days remain in the current year.

## Data Collection
This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## How It Works
- All calculations are performed locally in your browser
- No data leaves your device
- No analytics or tracking
- No third-party services
- No account or sign-in required

## Permissions
The extension uses only the `alarms` permission to update the badge counter periodically. This does not access any personal information.
